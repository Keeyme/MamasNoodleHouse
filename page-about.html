Szechuan Hot Pot

Szechuan Hot Pot is the most famous chinese hot pot. It is very popular and can be found in almost eery city in China. It is famous for its numbing and spicy flavor, resulting from a special seasoning Szechuon pepper (Hua Jiao) and other spicy ingredients like chili added to it, which marks its difference from other hot pots. In our restaurant, we create hot pot style dishes without using the hot pot. It is called hot pot style dish. These amazing dishes use only seasoning trom Szechuon. if yo want to challenge your taste buds, Szechuan hot pot style is highly recommended.
